Split an audio file into clips

Upload one longer recording (for example, a single audiobook chapter) and the app will split it at natural pauses into shorter clips. You'll get a preview to check before anything is saved — you can re-run at a different sensitivity or cancel. For best results split a book into chapters first and upload one chapter at a time.

Note: splitting runs on the Pi and can take a minute or two for a long file. Only one split or import can run at a time. The file must be under the upload size limit — if a chapter is too big, split the book into smaller chapters first.
