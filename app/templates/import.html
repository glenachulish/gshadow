Import from URL

Paste a learngaelic.scot URL and the audio will be downloaded, split, and turned into a new collection. Takes about 30–60 seconds.

Supported URL patterns:
https://learngaelic.scot/litir/litir.jsp?l=… (Litir)
https://learngaelic.scot/litirbheag/litir.jsp?l=… (Litir Bheag)
https://learngaelic.scot/look/look.jsp?b=… (Look @ LearnGaelic — video; audio extracted)
https://learngaelic.scot/watch/watch.jsp?v=… (Watch Gaelic — video; audio extracted)
For other audio, use the Mac splitter script and the New collection form.
